04 — Instagram Reel Formats

Reels are your
discovery engine

Goal: reach non-followers. Each reel should be 7–12 seconds, simple text, strong hook. Use these formats repeatedly — consistency beats novelty.

FORMAT / 01
Reasoning Question

Drops a question, shows options, ends with "comment your answer." Comments signal the algorithm to push your account to new audiences.

Govt Job Aspirants 👇 Can you solve this? [Question] [Options A / B / C / D] — last screen — Comment your answer 👇
Purpose → Comments = algorithm boost
FORMAT / 02
Hidden Job Opportunity

Opens with a surprising seat count nobody acts on. Then reveals salary, eligibility, deadline. Curiosity + low competition = high watch time.

This govt job has 400 seats and almost nobody applies. → ₹XX,000/month salary → Eligibility: [details] → Deadline: [date] Full details in bio.
Purpose → Saves + profile visits
FORMAT / 03
Salary Reveal

Lead with the number — it's what stops the scroll. Job role next, then eligibility and deadline. These reels perform very well.

₹52,000/month Govt Job 🏛️ → Role: [job title] → Eligibility: [details] → Deadline: [date] Apply link in bio →
Purpose → High shares, parent-driven virality
FORMAT / 04
Exam Shortcut

Show a reasoning or maths trick that saves time in exams. "Solve in 5 seconds." People save this before exams — highest save rate of any reel format.

Solve this reasoning question in 5 seconds ⚡ [Question] Shortcut method: [Step 1 → Step 2 → Answer] Save this before your exam 📌
Purpose → Saves + return visits
FORMAT / 05
Deadline Reminder

Urgency is your highest-converting trigger. Lead with the warning. Show the job. Make the cost of missing it feel real.

⚠ This govt job closes in 2 days. → Job: [title] → Seats: [number] → Eligibility: [details] Don't miss it → link in bio
Purpose → Urgency drives clicks

05 — Carousel Formats

Built for
saves

Saves are the highest-quality engagement signal on Instagram. These formats are designed to be bookmarked and returned to repeatedly.

CAROUSEL / 01
Top 5 Govt Jobs This Week

Slide 1: strong hook. Slides 2–6: one job per slide with key details. Last slide: CTA to site. Post every Monday. Becomes the content people bookmark and check weekly.

CAROUSEL / 02
Jobs for 10th / 12th Pass

Huge audience — most people assume govt jobs require degrees. This format unlocks possibilities they didn't know existed. Massive reach among younger followers and parents.

CAROUSEL / 03
Salary Breakdown

Grade III govt employee: basic pay + DA + HRA + total. Then pension, job security. The full picture, not just the headline number. Parents share this everywhere.

CAROUSEL / 04
Application Guide

How to apply for [specific recruitment]. Step-by-step. Mistakes to avoid. These get the highest save rates of any content format you will ever post.

06 — Facebook Playbook

Different audience,
different rules

Facebook users in NE India prefer text, lists, and alerts. They scroll slower, read fully, and share to family WhatsApp groups. Write for them.

FB FORMAT / 01
Weekly Job List

Text post, fully written out. Highly shareable. Becomes the Sunday content people forward to every family group.

Top Govt Jobs This Week 1. Assam Police – 1,645 posts 2. NTPC EET – 515 posts 3. Bank of Baroda – 166 posts 4. [Job 4] 5. [Job 5] Full details → necareerhub.in
FB FORMAT / 02
Deadline Alert

Urgency posts drive the highest click-through on Facebook. Short, clear, direct. Post at 8am and 7pm on deadline days.

⚠ Jobs Closing in 48 Hours • Assam Police • NTPC • PNB Apprentice Last chance to apply. Link in comments →
FB FORMAT / 03
Parent Targeted

Address parents directly. They forward everything. This is your most cost-effective distribution — free virality through family groups.

If your son or daughter is preparing for govt jobs, share this with them. [3 jobs opening this week] Deadline: [date] Apply: necareerhub.in

Your biggest advantage: you actually built a product. Most job pages just repost notifications. You can show alerts, reminders, filters — a real platform in action. That makes your content more credible than every competitor combined.

08 — Engagement Tactics

Always do
these three

Instagram pushes posts with active conversations. These habits take 2 minutes per post and meaningfully affect your reach.

TACTIC / 01
Pin a Comment

On every reel, pin a comment immediately after posting. Signals activity to the algorithm before organic engagement builds.

"Answer will be revealed tomorrow. Comment yours below 👇"
TACTIC / 02
Ask a Question

End every caption with one question. Comments are weighted higher than likes. One good question doubles your comment count.

"Are you preparing for SSC, Railway or State exams?"
TACTIC / 03
Reply Fast

Reply to every comment within the first hour of posting. Instagram treats your replies as additional comments. More comments = more reach.

"First 60 minutes after posting is the most important window for engagement."

10 — What Not to Do

Six habits that
kill accounts

Your brand is clear, useful information. These habits silently undo months of trust-building.

✕
400-word captions

Nobody reads them. Short, specific, scannable always wins.

✕
Motivational quotes

Generic inspiration does nothing for your niche. Zero saves, zero trust built.

✕
Too many hashtags

Hashtag stacking is 2018. Use 3–5 specific, relevant tags maximum.

✕
Posting PDFs

PDFs get no reach. Convert the same content into a carousel or reel.

✕
Generic news reposts

Anyone can copy-paste a notification. Add your context or don't post it.

✕
Going silent

2+ weeks of silence destroys months of trust. One post beats zero posts, always.
