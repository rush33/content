01 — Diagnosis

Where You Stand & Where the Opportunity Is

Before building, we need to be ruthlessly honest about the landscape. The government job niche in Northeast India is underserved digitally — most competitors are chaotic, ad-laden, and mobile-unfriendly. That's your moat. Here's the full picture.

📌 Core Insight: Job seekers in NE India are hungry for a trusted, clean, alert-driven source. Most are discovering jobs via WhatsApp forwards — which means they're already primed for notification-based products. You're solving a real pain point.

🎯

Your Core USPs

No ads, modern UI, personalised alerts, smart filters. These aren't features — they're trust signals.

⚡

Market Gap

Competitors like Naukri, Sarkari Result are national & cluttered. Nobody owns "NE government jobs" with a premium feel.

🔥

Audience Intent

High-intent, anxious searchers. They refresh daily. They share jobs in groups. Emotion = urgency + aspiration.

06 — Growth Tactics

The Moves That Compound

🔁

Cross-Platform Funneling

Every platform drives to website sign-ups for email/in-app alerts. Instagram → link in bio → sign up. Telegram → CTA in every post → sign up. The goal is always: get them into your alert system.

🤝

Local Micro-Influencer Collabs

Partner with NE-based coaching center YouTubers, APSC prep pages (5K–50K followers). Exchange: they mention NE Career Hub, you feature them in a post. No money needed initially.

🏆

Referral Contests

"Share this post + tag 2 friends who want govt jobs → Win ₹500 Amazon voucher." Runs quarterly. Explosive reach for very low cost. Use your existing users to recruit new ones.

📣

Group Seeding Strategy

Join top 20 Facebook Groups and Telegram groups for NE job seekers. Contribute genuine value (share real jobs). Over 60 days, you become the trusted source. Never spam — add value first.

📧

Email List as a Moat

Every social post should push toward email sign-up. A 10K email list is worth more than 50K Instagram followers. Offer a lead magnet: "Free: Complete NE Govt Job Calendar 2025 — PDF."

🔍

SEO-Driven YouTube

Title every video like a search query: "Assam Police Constable 2025 Apply Online" or "UPSC CAPF 2025 Notification NE India." These rank on YouTube AND Google Search for months.

07 — Monetization

5 Revenue Streams — No Compromise on UX

Your biggest USP is no annoying ads — never betray that. Here's how you monetize without compromising user trust. Start with streams 1–2, add others as you scale.

Stream 1
Premium Membership — ₹99–199/month

Offer a "Pro" tier: unlimited filters, earliest alerts (15min before public), resume review, document checklist templates, saved job folders. This is your primary revenue — low price, high volume, recurring.

Target: 500 members by Month 6
Stream 2
Coaching Center Partnerships

Local APSC/SSC coaching centers WANT access to your audience. Offer a sponsored "Recommended Courses" section on your website and occasional sponsored posts on social. Non-intrusive, trust-preserved.

₹5K–25K per sponsor/month
Stream 3
Digital Products

Sell high-quality PDFs: "Complete APSC Prelims Notes 2025," "NE Government Job Eligibility Master Guide," application form templates. One-time purchase. Promote heavily on social.

₹99–499 per product
Stream 4
Telegram Premium Access

Run a free public channel + a paid private group (₹49/month) where members get priority alerts 1 hour early, exclusive Q&A sessions with experts, and interview prep resources.

Low friction, recurring

Affiliate & Job Preparation Marketplace (Month 9+)

08 — 12-Month Roadmap

The Execution Timeline

Month 1–2 — Foundation
Build the Base
  • Set up all platforms with consistent branding (same handle, same logo, same bio)
  • Launch Telegram Channel — begin daily job posts immediately
  • Create 10 evergreen pieces of content before going live (carousel bank, reel templates)
  • Join 20 Facebook groups and Telegram groups as a genuine contributor
  • Set up email capture on website with free lead magnet (Job Calendar PDF)
  • Launch WhatsApp Channel
Month 3–4 — Ignition
Drive First 1000 Followers Per Platform
  • Run first referral contest (₹500 prize — cheap for the reach you'll get)
  • Reach out to 5 NE-based coaching center YouTube channels for cross-promotion
  • Launch YouTube Shorts — repurpose Instagram Reels with searchable titles
  • Start ₹200/day Facebook Ads targeting Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya job seekers
  • Post first "Success Story" feature — even if you need to reach out manually to find one
  • Launch monthly "NE Jobs Report" — a shareable summary that positions you as an authority
Month 5–6 — Monetize
Turn Audience into Revenue
  • Launch Pro Membership — target 100 early adopters with launch discount (₹59/month for first 100)
  • Pitch first coaching center sponsor — package: website feature + 4 social posts/month
  • Release first paid digital product (₹199 APSC guide)
  • Launch paid Telegram group alongside free channel
  • Review all metrics, double down on top-performing content types
Month 7–12 — Scale
Compound, Systematize, Expand
  • Hire a part-time content assistant (₹5–8K/month) to handle daily posting
  • Launch affiliate program (Testbook, Adda247 partnerships)
  • Expand to cover Central Government jobs more comprehensively
  • Run quarterly giveaways to maintain growth momentum
  • Consider a dedicated mobile app if website traffic exceeds 50K monthly visitors
  • Build "NE Career Hub Ambassador" program — 1 person per state who shares content locally

10 — Start Monday

Your First 7 Days Action Plan

Day 1
Set up all platform handles with identical branding. Write a clear bio: "🇮🇳 Govt job alerts for NE India | No ads | Smart filters | necareerhub.com" — use this on every platform.
Day 2
Launch Telegram Channel. Post 5 jobs immediately. Invite every contact personally. Share the link on all platforms.
Day 3
Create 5 Instagram Reel templates in Canva. Use your brand colors. Batch-create content for 2 weeks. Consistency matters more than perfection early on.
Day 4
Join 10 Facebook groups about NE government jobs. Don't post yet — read, understand the audience, see what questions repeat.
Day 5
Create your lead magnet — a simple PDF: "NE Government Job Calendar 2025 — All Upcoming Recruitment." Add email capture form to website homepage. This is your most important long-term asset.
Day 6
Post your first Instagram Reel. "Top 5 Government Jobs in NE India this week." Upload to YouTube Shorts same day. Cross-post to Facebook. Announce on Telegram.
Day 7
Review Day 1–6. What got traction? Double down. Set up a simple content scheduling tool (Buffer or Meta Business Suite). Plan week 2. Commit to showing up every single day for 90 days — that's all it takes to win this niche.
